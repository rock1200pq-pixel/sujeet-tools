Our Story

About Rock Tools

We believe powerful tools should be free, private, and accessible to everyone — no strings attached.

🚀
Our Mission

Tools For Everyone.

Rock Tools was built on one belief — that essential file tools should be free, simple, and work without putting your privacy at risk.

Too many tools online force you to create accounts, upload your private files to unknown servers, hit paywalls for basic features, or bombard you with ads. We built Rock Tools to be the opposite of all that.

Every tool runs entirely in your browser using modern Web APIs. Your files never leave your device. There's no server, no database, no tracking — just you and your files.

What We Stand For

Our Values

Four principles that guide every decision we make.

🔒
Privacy First
Your files are yours alone. We process everything locally — nothing is ever uploaded, stored, or shared.
⚡
Speed Matters
Browser-based processing is instant. No waiting for uploads, server queues, or email delivery of results.

Accessible to All

Free, Always
